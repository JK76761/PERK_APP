# Perk Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-18

## Overview

Perk is a savings discovery app that helps users find verified offers, education pricing, loyalty programs, transport concessions, and related money-saving opportunities.

Perk is designed to work without a mandatory user account in the current release.

## Data Perk stores

Perk currently stores a limited amount of app data locally on the device:

- saved perk IDs
- recommendation preferences
- discovery region preferences
- notification category preferences

This local storage is used to support the in-app experience and does not create an online user profile.

## Data Perk does not currently collect

Perk does not currently collect or require:

- username and password login data
- payment card information
- government ID or passport details
- personal banking credentials
- user-posted community content

## Third-party services

Perk may use third-party services to deliver app functionality:

- Supabase: used to read verified public perk listings
- Logo.dev: used to display optional brand logos when available
- External provider websites: opened when a user chooses to view or claim an offer

These services may receive technical request information such as IP address, user agent, device metadata, or network metadata as part of normal app usage.

## External links

Perk may open official provider websites in the device browser. Those external websites operate under their own privacy policies, cookies, and terms.

## Children and eligibility

Perk is intended for general informational use by students, early-career users, and other people exploring verified savings opportunities. Individual perks may have their own age, residency, student verification, or eligibility requirements.

## Data retention

Saved perks and local preference settings remain on the device unless the user removes them, resets app data, or uninstalls the app.
