## Short description draft

Perk is a verified savings discovery app built for students and early-career savers. Browse official-source offers across banking, food, transport, software, subscriptions, loyalty rewards, and more.

## Full description draft

Perk helps you discover verified ways to save money without digging through random promo pages.

Use Perk to browse official-source perks across:

- banking bonuses
- food savings
- transport concessions
- software and study tools
- subscriptions
- rewards programs
- research and earn-money opportunities

Why Perk feels different:

- verified offers from official provider sources
- clean detail pages with direct source links
- saved perks stored locally on your device
- support for students and early-career savers
- helpful discovery across Australia-first and global offers

Perk is designed to help you find practical savings, not just coupon clutter.

## Suggested keywords

student discounts, savings, cashback, banking offers, food deals, rewards, subscriptions, verified offers, study tools

## Review notes draft

Perk is an information and discovery app. It helps users browse verified offers and savings opportunities, then opens the provider’s official website to view or claim the offer. The app does not process payments or require account login in the current release.
