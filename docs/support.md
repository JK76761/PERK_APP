## What to include in a support email

If you are reporting a problem, it helps to include:

- device model
- iOS version
- app version
- the perk or provider involved
- what happened
- screenshots if relevant

## Common requests

Perk support can help with:

- outdated offer reports
- broken provider links
- logo or branding issues
- perk requests
- business or partnership inquiries

## Response scope

Perk aims to review verified offer issues and provider-link problems as quickly as possible, but response time can vary depending on workload and the type of request.
